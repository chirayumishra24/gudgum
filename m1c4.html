The Origin Story

Discover how GudGum was founded to revolutionize sustainable chewing gum.

GudGum’s Beginnings

Every great venture has an inspiring origin story, and GudGum is no different. Founded in Bengaluru, India, this eco-friendly gum brand emerged from a passionate desire to change the way we think about everyday products. The creators recognized that while gum is a beloved treat worldwide, its environmental impact often goes unnoticed.

Watch the Origin Story

The Idea

The founders of GudGum saw a world overflowing with non-biodegradable waste, particularly from conventional candy wrappers and synthetic ingredients. Driven by their love for the planet and a commitment to sustainability, they set out to create a gum that was not only enjoyable but also kind to the Earth. This vision was the spark that ignited the journey towards an innovative, eco-friendly product.

The Journey

Launching a sustainable brand comes with unique challenges. The founders faced numerous obstacles, including:

  • Sourcing sustainable, biodegradable materials for gum and packaging.
  • Balancing eco-friendly production with cost-effectiveness.
  • Educating consumers about the environmental impact of traditional gum.

Despite the hurdles, the team was relentless. They focused on research and development, exploring various natural formulations and biodegradable materials, leading to the birth of GudGum.
