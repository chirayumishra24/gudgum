Marketing Strategy

GudGum’s story glows with purpose. Its marketing weaves sustainability and fun to connect with eco-conscious youth.

Marketing Approach

  • Storytelling: Highlights plastic-free mission and tribal farmer support, resonating with 75% of consumers valuing social impact
  • Visuals & Wordplay: Pastel tones, eco-themed designs (leaves, trees), and taglines like “Good for you. Gud for Earth.”
