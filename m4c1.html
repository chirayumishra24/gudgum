GudGum: Meet the Team and How We Make Our Gum

Welcome to GudGum!

GudGum is India’s first plastic-free, 100% natural chewing gum, made with real chicle and recyclable packaging. Our mission is to create tasty, sustainable gum that’s good for you and the planet. Meet the awesome team behind GudGum and see how we turn natural ingredients into your favorite gum!

Our Founders

Mayank and Bhuvan Nagori started GudGum in 2022 to make chewing gum fun, healthy, and eco-friendly. They dream up new ideas, plan how GudGum grows, and make sure every piece of gum is plastic-free and super tasty. Their big goal? A world with zero plastic waste!

Supply Chain: Sourcing the Good Stuff

 

Our supply chain team finds natural chicle (a tree sap) and 100% biodegradable packaging. They work with farmers and suppliers to make sure everything is eco-friendly and high-quality, so your gum is good for the planet from start to finish.

R&D: Making Gum Yummier

 

Our research and development (R&D) team creates cool flavors like mango chili and makes our gum chewy and fun. They test new ideas to keep GudGum natural and delicious, improving the texture based on feedback from fans like you!

Behind the Scenes: From Tree to Store

Here’s how we make GudGum, from nature to your pocket:

Chicle Harvesting: We get natural chicle from sapodilla trees, a sustainable gum base that’s 100% plastic-free.
Mixing Flavors: Our R&D team blends natural flavors (like mango chili) and sweeteners for a tasty, healthy chew.
Eco-Packaging: We pack the gum in Recyclable ziplocks, keeping the planet clean.
Quality Checks: Every batch is tested to make sure it’s safe, chewy, and delicious.
Shipping to Stores: We send GudGum to shops and online stores, ready for you to enjoy!
