Brand Personality

Discover the playful, eco-conscious vibe of GudGum.

Chew Gud, Feel Gud, Do Gud!

GudGum’s vibe is all about being playful, eco-conscious, and totally approachable. Our mantra, “Chew Gud, Feel Gud, Do Gud,” captures a brand that’s fun to chew, kind to the planet, and easy to love. With earthy greens, browns, and natural imagery like bark and leaves, we connect with the 55% of consumers who vibe with paper-based packaging.

Chewing Gum the Way It Was Meant to Be!

GudGum is used to bring freshness to your mouth and combat bad breath, and could be used as an after-meal mouth freshener. It also brings back the ancient tradition of chewing natural tree sap, making it fun, sustainable, and kind to the planet.

Mission: Deliver natural, sustainable gum that sparks joy.
Vision: A planet where every chew supports eco-friendly living.
Values: Playful, green, and community-driven.

Why Packaging Matters

Does the look of a box really sway buyers? You bet! A 2025 McKinsey report shows 55% of consumers prefer paper-based packaging for its eco-friendly vibe. And it’s not just about looks—43% say sustainable packaging makes them more likely to buy again, even if taste is king. GudGum’s natural imagery and recyclable ziplock make every chew a planet-positive choice.

Key Takeaways

A clear vision and mission align GudGum with consumer values, with 78% prioritizing sustainability.
Natural ingredients and recyclable packaging set GudGum apart in a synthetic-heavy market.
A playful, purposeful brand personality builds emotional connections, encouraging eco-conscious choices.
