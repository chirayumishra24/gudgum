Create Your Own Product and Customer Persona

Objective

Develop a Product based customer persona for GudGum’s eco-conscious Indian youth audience to inform marketing strategies.

Tool

Below you can find the Product Pathfinder Lab (free, browser-based, export word file Report).

Description

Interns create a customer persona for your product's target audience ( example: Eco Bottle for eco-conscious Indian youth, aged 18-25, urban, sustainability-focused) using Product Pathfinder Lab’s interface. The persona should include demographics, behaviors, preferences, and motivations to guide marketing strategies for your sustainable product. The activity simulates a real-world marketing task for your sustainable plastic-free mission.

Steps to Complete Assignment

LMS Activity

  • Upload word files to below Choose File.
  • Share descriptions in discussion boards for feedback.

Outputs

  • Downloadable: Word file or PNG/JPG screenshot of the Product Persona final result.
  • Sharable: Screenshots or descriptions for peer review.
  • Experiential Value: Simulates driven market research, teaching persona development and marketing strategy skills.

Why It Fits

Product Pathfinder Lab’s interface offers a free, user-friendly platform to generate detailed customer personas from text prompts, aligning with GudGum’s marketing goals.
