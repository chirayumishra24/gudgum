SkilliZee & GudGum Capstone Project

Overview Task 1

Welcome to the final capstone project of the SkilliZee EcoPreneurship Internship with GudGum! This project is your chance to showcase your creativity, sustainability knowledge, and entrepreneurial skills by creating a website, developing a business model, producing a pitching video, and reflecting on your internship journey. Your work will help amplify GudGum’s mission to make plastic-free chewing gum the future!

Theme: Be Gud. Be Bold. Be the Voice.
First Step: Download the template and fill that out.
Final Step: Upload the Document filled with your answers.
Hashtag: #BeGudWithGudGum

The task is Mandatory for: Internship Certificate and Badge *

Download Task Template

 

Project Tasks

Task 1: Create a Website Using Gamma

Design a simple, visually appealing website using Gamma to promote GudGum’s eco-friendly mission. Highlight its unique flavors, sustainable packaging, and appeal to young audiences.

  • Include at least 3 sections: About GudGum, Why Plastic-Free Matters, and Fun Flavors.
  • Use vibrant visuals and a clean layout inspired by GudGum’s branding.
  • Submit the Gamma website link via the SkilliZee LMS.
 

Submission & Rewards

Submission Steps:

  • Download the template.
  • Enter the required details in the document.
  • Paste the published Gamma website link in the document.
  • List the features you added in the website.
  • Save the document.
  • Upload the document to the SkilliZee LMS using the default ‘Choose File’ button at the bottom of the page.
  • Select the saved document in the LMS.
  • Write a note (max 100 characters) in the LMS.
  • Click ‘Submit’ in the LMS.

 

Rewards:

  • Complete all Assignments and Quizes: Recieve your Certificates and Badges
  • Top 10 Projects: Featured on SkilliZee and GudGum feeds, earn a “Changemaker Badge,” and get an exclusive invite to the next-level entrepreneurship module.
  • Campaign Amplification: SkilliZee and GudGum will create a reel featuring the best student projects for media/PR.

Support & Resources

To help you succeed, SkilliZee provides:

  • Optional Canva templates for Gamma website design.

Mentor Message: “Hi, I’m Astrid. Your #BeGudWithGudGum project is your chance to shine! Be authentic, be bold, and inspire the world to go plastic-free with GudGum! 🌍”

  • Please upload the assignment and answer all questions accordingly.
