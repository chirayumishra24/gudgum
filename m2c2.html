Ingredient Insight: What is Xylitol?

“Xylitol is derived from birch trees and corn cobs — it’s totally plant-based and even helps reduce plaque. Unlike sugar, it’s safe for your teeth and sweetens naturally.”
— Mayank Nagori, Co-founder of GudGum

GudGum is More Than Just a Product

GudGum is an idea — one that inspires people to:

Think before they chew.
Choose planet-positive alternatives.
Support sustainable, eco-conscious brands.

India-First Innovation

GudGum sources its chicle from local tribal farmers, supporting livelihoods and ensuring social sustainability is at the heart of the supply chain. It’s not just green — it’s fair.

The Mayan people have been chewing gum made of tree sap for thousands of years. Chewing GudGum is not only better for the planet and you, but also helps support the livelihoods of tribal farmers who harvest this tree sap.
