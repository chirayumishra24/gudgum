Customer Journey Overview

How do customers discover, buy, and love GudGum? From catching their eye on social media to tossing the wrapper in wet waste, every step of the customer journey is designed to be seamless, eco-conscious, and fun. Let’s explore how GudGum connects with you!

Awareness

GudGum grabs attention through Instagram, Shark Tank, and influencer partnerships. Catchy posts, cool pitches, and trusted voices spread our eco-friendly, playful vibe to green-loving chewers everywhere.

Purchase

Buying GudGum is a breeze! Our seamless e-commerce checkout offers cash-on-delivery for convenience, making it easy for you to grab your favorite gum online and start chewing.

Post-Purchase

GudGum’s eco-friendly packaging is designed for easy recyclable, and 78% of consumers check recycling labels, loving brands that make green choices simple.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

Most chewing gums come in plastic or foil, but GudGum says no way! We use paper that break down naturally and recyclable ziplock, keeping the planet clean. Our simple, clean wrapper design spreads awareness about sustainability.

  • Paper wrappers decompose naturally, unlike plastic.
  • Recyclable materials reduce environmental impact.
  • Clean designs inspire eco-conscious choices.
