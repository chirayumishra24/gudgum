Inside GudGum:

Building a Sustainable Brand from Chew to Change

Hello, future eco-entrepreneurs! We’re thrilled to welcome you to this exciting journey into sustainable branding with GudGum, India’s pioneering eco-friendly chewing gum brand leading the charge in sustainable confectionery.

Below is an Interactive Video. Please watch the video and Interact with it click on options appearing in the video.

What You Will Learn

Dive deep into the world of sustainable branding with hands-on insights:

  • Environmental Challenges: Explore the problems caused by conventional products and the importance of sustainable alternatives.
  • GudGum’s Identity: Analyze how GudGum’s mission, vision, and unique brand identity set it apart in the marketplace.
  • Innovative Product Development: Discover the role of natural ingredient sourcing and biodegradable packaging in creating eco-conscious products.
  • Strategic Market Insights: Evaluate the competitive landscape and learn to develop marketing strategies that promote sustainability.
  • Hands-On Experience: Create your own branding and marketing materials that reflect GudGum’s eco-conscious mission.
