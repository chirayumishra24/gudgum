GudGum: Challenges in Building a Sustainable Business

Challenges Faced by GudGum

Building a sustainable business like GudGum isn’t easy! From production hurdles to market preferences, GudGum faces real-world challenges in bringing its eco-friendly, plastic-free gum to conscious chewers. Let’s explore the obstacles and how they shape GudGum’s journey.

Manufacturing Scale

Small-scale production keeps GudGum’s quality top-notch but drives up costs. This makes it tough to scale operations and compete with larger gum brands that benefit from economies of scale.

Sourcing Materials

Finding sustainable chicle, the natural base for GudGum, is a challenge. Its scarcity leads to inconsistent supply, which can disrupt production and limit the ability to meet growing demand.

Customer Education

Many consumers prefer cheaper, plastic-based gums over sustainable options. A big hurdle is that 46% of consumers cite unclear labeling as a barrier, making it hard for GudGum to communicate its eco-friendly benefits effectively.

Price Sensitivity

In India’s price-sensitive market, GudGum’s premium pricing (₹350–₹1,584) faces resistance. Convincing consumers to pay more for sustainable, high-quality gum is an ongoing challenge.
